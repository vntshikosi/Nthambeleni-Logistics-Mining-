About Nthambeleni

Our Story & Our Vision

Our Company

Nthambeleni Logistics is a trusted, proudly South African transport and logistics partner, operating a modern fleet that services clients nationwide and across the SADC region. We were founded to meet a growing need for reliable, safe, and customer-focused freight solutions in a fast-changing economy.

For us, logistics is not simply about moving cargo from one point to another; it's about building trust, strengthening supply chains, and delivering value at every stage of the journey.

Helping the Mining Sector

We understand that in mining, downtime equals lost revenue. Nthambeleni Logistics provides robust support for the mining industry by ensuring the timely transport of "Yellow Metal" equipment (excavators, graders) and bulk mineral haulage. We handle the heavy lifting so mines can focus on extraction and production.

Empowering Logistics Needs

Whether you are a small supplier or a large corporation, our logistics network is designed to be flexible. We manage road freight compliance, route planning, and abnormal loads. Our goal is to reduce your transport risks and costs while ensuring your goods arrive safely and on time.

Our Values

  • Integrity & Honesty: We conduct every transaction with fairness.
  • Transparency: Keeping clients informed at every stage.
  • Creativity: Finding smart solutions for unique challenges.
